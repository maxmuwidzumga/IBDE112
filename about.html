About Us

Title One

The Northern Lights Bed & Breakfast is a charming bed and breakfast (B&B) located in Cyberspace, outside of Main, WWW. The location is well known for its beauty and concentration of spectacular views of the night sky and gorgeous displays of the Aurora Borealis. The B&B resides in our newly renovated home. Each of the three rooms have their own private bath. The facility has a wonderful centralized living room for the socialization of the guests, a private garden patio, and an on-site orchard upon our 10 acres of land.
