Wedding Suite

The only suite with 24/7 hot tub access! The finest quality workmanship can be seen in every detail of this "sound-proofed" corner view suite that is closest to the water and also has its own exterior entrance to ensure maximum privacy.

A signature king-size bed with down pillows, down duvet and feather-bed will reward you with the most comfortable sleep imaginable - we gladly switch out the feather and down when requested.

Garden Suite

This gorgeous suite is tastefully furnished with a comfy bed, duvet and throws. It is a very large suite which consist of a double bedroom (King size bed or two singles) with modern en suite bathroom (with bath).

A 42" satellite TV and a gas fireplace are featured in this guest suite. A patio boasts garden and ocean views. A private entrance offers added convenience.

Price per night: $500 for a 2 night stay

Family Suite

This suite was built for families looking for comfort and space. Challenged with being both a honeymoon and family destination, we built the family suite and honeymoon suite are at opposite ends of the building. Additionally, the entire building exceeds all building code "sound-proofing" specifications.

A signature queen bed plus 2 twins with down pillows and down duvets will ensure a good night's rest. Read your favourite book or watch your favourite show or DVD on the wide-screen Samsung HDTV.

Price per night: $700 for a 2 night stay
